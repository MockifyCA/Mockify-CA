AIM

Built to support smarter SPOM preparation

Mockify CA helps students prepare for SPOM with a cleaner, more focused practice flow built around how the exam is actually approached.

Mockify is designed to help students prepare for SPOM through case-based questions that feel closer to actual exam thinking. Instead of passive reading, students can practice in a more exam-like environment and build confidence with the format they are expected to handle.

With timed practice, better question flow, and a more structured experience, Mockify CA helps students improve time management, stay consistent with preparation, and approach SPOM with greater clarity and control.
