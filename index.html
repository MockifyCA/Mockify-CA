Why Case-Based Practice Matters
01 — EXAM NATURE

SPOM is Application-Heavy

ICAI does not test memory. It tests your ability to apply law and concepts to real business scenarios under time pressure. Rote learning will not work here.

02 — QUESTION DESIGN

MCQs Are Deceptively Tricky

All four options are designed to appear plausible. The correct answer depends entirely on reading the case carefully — one missed detail changes everything.

03 — TIME MANAGEMENT

3 Hours Goes Faster Than You Think

Most students run short on time in the actual exam. Practicing under strict CBT conditions builds the speed and composure needed on exam day.
